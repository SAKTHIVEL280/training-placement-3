1. Smart Parking Slot Management System

Scenario:
A parking lot has N slots. Cars arrive and leave at different times.

Requirements:

Allocate the lowest available slot number to a car

Prevent parking if the lot is full

Allow car exit and free the slot

Display current occupied slots

Key Challenges (Not Basic):

Track dynamic state

Handle reuse of freed slots

Avoid duplicate allocations


2. Bank Account with Transaction History

Scenario:
Simulate a bank account that supports:

Deposit

Withdrawal

Balance check

Viewing last 5 transactions

Rules:

No withdrawal beyond balance

Maintain ordered transaction history

Old transactions should drop off after 5

Key Challenges:

Maintaining rolling history

Input-driven flow

Data consistency


3. Online Exam Evaluation System

Scenario:
An exam has 10 questions with predefined answers.

Requirements:

Accept student answers

Auto-evaluate and calculate score

Track attempted vs unattempted

Assign grade based on score

Twist (Important):

Negative marking for wrong answers

Skip questions allowed

Key Challenges:

Parallel data handling (answers vs responses)

Conditional scoring logic

Display inventory summary

Key Challenges:

Multiple product handling

Constraint enforcement

Conditional alerts
